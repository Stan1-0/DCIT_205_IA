Prom Night

We are excited to announce a groundbreaking event that has never happened before at the University of Ghana! The Computer Science Students Association and Actuarial Science & Statistics Society are coming together for the FIRST-EVER COLLABORATIVE dinner and awards night! Ladies let's bring out our elegance and style with your finest dresses or chic ensembles. Gentlemen, it's time to showcase your dapper charm with well-fitted suits dress shirts and trousers. Mark your calendars and get ready to mingle with your like-minded peers in an enjoyable and intellectually stimulating environment!!!! Looking forward to seeing you all!!!!!

COMPSSA ADVENTURE TRIP!

Hey students! Are you ready for an unforgettable adventure? COMPSSA is organizing a fantastic trip to Tafi Monkey Sanctuary, Afadjato (the tallest mountain in Ghana), and Wli Waterfalls on August 16th, right after our exams. Join us for just 250 cedis! Let's conquer Afadjato, meet playful monkeys at Tafi Monkey Sanctuary, and enjoy the beauty of Wli Waterfalls! Spread the word, and bring your friends along - the more, the merrier! Don't miss this incredible journey with COMPSSA! See you there!
